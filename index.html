History

Welcome to ChillScoop Ice Cream, where every scoop is a delightful journey into sweetness! Our shop was established in 2024, born out of a passion for creating delicious and imaginative ice cream flavors that bring joy to our community. Located in the heart of Marinduque, we've been serving up smiles one scoop at a time to locals and visitors alike.

Mission

We also believe in giving back to our community. ChillScoop Ice Cream actively supports local events, charities, and schools, because we know that a strong community is the key to success. We host ice cream socials, fundraisers, and other special events to bring people together and share the joy of ice cream.

About

Our story began with a simple love for ice cream and a desire to create a space where people of all ages could come together to enjoy quality frozen treats. We believe in using the finest ingredients, locally sourced whenever possible, to craft unique and mouthwatering flavors that you won't find anywhere else. From classic vanilla and chocolate to exotic flavors inspired by global cuisines, there's something for everyone at ChillScoop Ice Cream. What sets us apart is our commitment to innovation and creativity. Our team is constantly experimenting with new flavor combinations, ensuring that every visit to our shop is a fresh and exciting experience. Whether you're in the mood for a traditional cone, a decadent sundae, or a refreshing milkshake, we've got you covered.
